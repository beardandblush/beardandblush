5 Things you need to know before choosing your makeup artist

Akash, july-12-2019

People are so precautious nowadays about everything and when it’s our body we add an extra layer of precaution to it. People say normal is beautiful but at the same time they add make up to their glory to look more attractive. We don’t compromise over small things when your hard earned money is involved then why to choose anyone when it comes to cosmetics and stuffs.

Here are the 5 things you must know before choosing your makeup artist.

  • Having high knowledge on makeup and cosmetology: You will find a lot of makeup artists practising without having proper education on cosmetology. It is highly recommended to avoid such artists as they won’t have proper knowledge neither about the product substance nor your skin types. An ideal artist must have good knowledge about the product substances and their probable effects on your skin.
  • Experience and portfolio: This is a very important factor while choosing your artist. Make sure he/she has served enough client. Look for genuine portfolios (Avoid edited or retouched portfolio images).
  • Keeping proper hygiene: It is very very important to maintain proper hygiene while practising makeup or cosmetology work. Make you the place and equipments are well maintained.
  • Customer oriented: A lot of time you will see artists are not giving enough time on a single client as they are planning to serve others at the same time. I‌ will say this is nothing but a sign of greed. An idea artist will invest enough time on their client and make sure they are happy with the service.
  • Cost saving: How much an artist will charge may depend on many factors. But it is advised for the client to do a research on the products so that they can assume how much the service should take.
