Our Story

"Making ones skin reflect beauty is one of the purest form of art and without being creative and knowledgeable, it is hard to shine brightly in this field."

Beard and Blush, a cosmetology clinic and salon founded by Cosmetologist Madhushree Ghosh in the year of 2019. Currently we are operating in agartala, tripura and planning to launch our new branches in mumbai and kolkata soon.

Madhushree Ghosh is having masters in cosmetology including hair dressing, spa therapy, professional and media makeup. After working in mumbai as a cosmetologist and makeup artist for a long time, madhushree decied to serve in her hometown.

"I was always interested in colors, art, beauty and all these creative things. Naturally I loved being in this profession where I could explore and express creativity on a daily basis. After gettin my masters when I visited many salons in my hometown, I realised there is a big vaccum. I felt I can provide better service to the people of agartala and without a second thought I jumped into it." - Madhushree Ghosh

Beard & Blush is cosmetology clinic where we hand picked our artists. We strongly focus on professionalism and knowledge. We are having speciality on Makeup, Hair, Skin, Nail, Eye lash etc.

All our managing and operations are handled by Mr. Gourab Biswas (COO Beard & Blush). For queries regarding franchise please connect with him.

We will welcome you with open arms to give Beard & Blush a try, hopefully this will be the last salon stop you will ever need.
